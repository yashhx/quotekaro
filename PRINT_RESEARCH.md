# Print-shop field notes (researched 2026-07-12)

Cheat-sheet for demos/meetings with printing company owners. Sources: 2024-25 web
research on Indian flex/signage + digital/offset shops. Rates are typical local-market
INR; metro premium shops run higher, wholesale markets lower.

## The daily chaos (what the tracker solves)
- Orders arrive as WhatsApp JPGs/"design bhejo"; 72dpi photos as "designs"; CDR is king.
- Corrections AFTER approval (names/phone numbers); wedding cards worst - whole family reviews.
- Same-day rush is normal: order by noon -> deliver evening (birthday/muhurat/shok sabha).
- Advance-baki cycle: 30-50% advance, balance "delivery pe" that stretches weeks; khata customers.
- "Same as last time" reorders depend on finding the old CDR file.
- Content-pending stalls: job logged, advance taken, matter never arrives - customer blames shop.
- Inter-shop outsourcing: digital shop -> offset press for cards/bill books; lamination outsourced.
- Season spikes: wedding Nov-Feb/Apr-May, school Apr-Jul (ID cards from messy Excel+photos), Diwali boxes Aug-Sep booking.
- A day = 8-20 jobs: many Rs 200-1,500 quick jobs + 1-2 anchor jobs (Rs 3k-15k). Avg tracked order ~Rs 3,000. Viable micro shop = Rs 1-3 lakh/month.
- MIS vendor pitch stat: ~5 missed WhatsApp orders/month x Rs 3,000 avg = Rs 1.8 lakh/yr lost - use this line in demos.
