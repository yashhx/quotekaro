Questions

Asked by real owners.

I already quote in Excel. Why do I need this?
Keep quoting in Excel — seriously. QuoteKaro's job starts after the quote goes out: log it in 30 seconds (or import your sheet), and now every pending rupee has a status and a follow-up date. Excel makes quotes; QuoteKaro makes sure they come back as orders.
Does it work on my phone?
Yes — it's built phone-first and installs from the browser (Add to Home Screen), no app store needed. Big text, big buttons, readable numbers. Works on any Android or iPhone.
How does the WhatsApp integration actually work?
Two layers. Out of the box: every quote, follow-up and chase opens WhatsApp with the message pre-written — you just hit send from your own number. Connected mode: link a WhatsApp Business number and incoming customer messages (text, photos, PDFs) appear inside the app automatically, ready to log as quotes with one tap.
Is my data safe? Can my competitors see my rates?
Your rates, customers and quotes are yours alone — never shown to any other shop, never used against you. And with one-tap Excel/CSV export, your data can always leave with you. No lock-in.
Does it calculate my machining time?
No — and be suspicious of anything that claims to. You know your machines; you supply the cycle time. QuoteKaro does the part that leaks money: true hourly rates, setup spread over quantity, overhead, margin, GST — instantly and consistently.
