⚙️ CNC / MACHINE SHOP

Quote with real costs. Then watch every machine earn.

Gut-feel rates lose money quietly. Idle machines lose it loudly. Both end here.

  • True costing — material + machine time + labour + overheads + margin. You give the cycle time, it does the money math: ₹51.00 + ₹54.90 + ₹12.33, +18% overhead, +25% margin = ₹174.39/pc. Every paisa accounted.
  • Machine floor — every machine's job, % done, time left, finish time — with a +8% breakdown buffer. Breakdown? Pause, move the remaining pieces to a free machine, and the math redoes itself.
  • True hourly rate — depreciation, power, operator, maintenance: know what one machine-hour really costs (a VMC 850 ≈ ₹366/hr) before you quote it.

Seedhi baat

Mere clients Excel format mangte hain. Phir?

Perfect — keep making quotes wherever you like. TrackRakho takes 30 seconds to log each one, and imports/exports Excel files directly. It's the tracker on top of your existing habit, not a replacement for it.

Does the app calculate my machining time?

No — you know your machines better than any software. You give the cycle time; the app does the money math and the tracking, honestly and instantly.

Staff ko sikhana padega?

If they can use WhatsApp, they can use this. Big text, plain language, three languages — designed for the shop floor, not for a software office.

Is my data safe?

Your data belongs to you. It lives on your phone and in your own cloud account, and you can export everything to Excel any time. No lock-in.
